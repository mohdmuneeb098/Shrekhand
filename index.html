Our Story

The secret behind the creamy goodness.

SHREKHAND was born out of a desire to put a modern twist on a classic dessert. We take the traditional process and infuse it with bold, new flavors that are perfect for today's palate. Each batch is made with the freshest ingredients and a whole lot of love.

We believe that dessert should be an experience. Our mission is to deliver a taste that's both nostalgic and exciting, a flavor journey that you'll want to share with everyone. Welcome to the SHREKHAND family!
