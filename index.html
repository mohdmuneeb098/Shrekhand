Meet Our Team

Our Story

Brand Story, Mission & Values

At Shrikhand, our journey began with a simple belief: that tradition and luxury can co-exist beautifully. We are a family of artisans dedicated to preserving the rich heritage of Indian sweets, starting with the beloved Shrikhand. Our mission is to elevate this classic dessert from a simple treat to a gourmet experience, using only the finest ingredients sourced with care and crafted with love. We value authenticity, quality, and the joy that comes from a perfect dessert.

What Makes Our Shrikhand Unique?

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to quality. Our shrikhand is made from a special, hand-strained yogurt that gives it an unparalleled creamy consistency. We use pure saffron and authentic Indian spices, staying true to traditional recipes while incorporating modern, hygienic techniques. It's a taste of home, perfected with a modern twist.
